# StaySync Blueprint

## Overview

StaySync is a modern hotel booking application designed to provide a seamless and intuitive user experience. It allows users to search for available rooms, view room details, and make reservations. The application will also include an admin panel for managing rooms, bookings, and staff.

## Features

*   **User Authentication:** Secure user registration and login.
*   **Room Search:** A powerful search form to find available rooms based on dates, guest count, and room type.
*   **Room Listings:** A beautiful and informative display of available rooms.
*   **Booking System:** A simple and secure booking process.
*   **Admin Panel:** A comprehensive dashboard for managing rooms, bookings, and staff.
*   **Responsive Design:** A mobile-first design that works on all devices.

## Style and Design

*   **Color Palette:** A modern and inviting color palette with a mix of warm and cool tones. The primary colors will be a deep blue and a vibrant coral, with shades of gray for backgrounds and text.
*   **Typography:** Clean and legible fonts. A serif font will be used for headings to give a touch of elegance, while a sans-serif font will be used for body text for readability.
*   **Iconography:** Modern and intuitive icons to enhance user experience.
*   **Layout:** A clean and spacious layout with a focus on usability.
